# Candidate scoring rubric

Use this rubric when the task is candidate assessment.

## Suggested dimensions
- Domain relevance
- Functional depth
- Leadership or ownership level
- Evidence quality
- Motivation fit
- Compensation fit
- Location fit
- Risk flags

## Output pattern
Return:
1. overall recommendation: strong yes / conditional yes / maybe / no
2. 3-5 match points
3. 3-5 risk points
4. follow-up interview questions
5. suggested next step

## Warning
Do not overstate certainty when evidence is weak. Make the gaps visible.
